About STACKLY

We're building the most engaging short-video platform for creators worldwide. Orange is our color, community is our soul.

who we are

INSTYPE was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: make video creation fun, social, and rewarding. We're a team of 50+ passionate creators, engineers, and dreamers based in Mumbai and San Francisco.

Our platform empowers millions to express themselves through short reels, connect with like-minded people, and build communities around their passions.
